Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9×5 inch loaf pan.

Mix mashed bananas and melted butter. Stir in sugar, egg, and vanilla. Sprinkle baking soda and salt over the mixture and stir. Add flour and mix until just combined. Pour into the pan.

Bake for about 60 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a rack.
